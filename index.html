The Ladder of Engagement

Theory, application & brand analysis

Understanding the Ladder of Engagement

The Ladder of Engagement is a framework that describes the progressive depth of consumer involvement with a brand's digital content — from passive consumption through to active advocacy. Rooted in Godin's (1999) permission marketing philosophy, the model recognises that sustainable brand relationships are built incrementally rather than through interruptive mass communication.

Platform critique using academic framework

How marketers use TikTok

TikTok has emerged as a dominant short-form video platform with over one billion monthly active users globally. For marketers, the platform offers access to highly engaged audiences through native content formats including in-feed video, branded hashtag challenges, and TikTok LIVE. Its algorithm-driven discovery mechanism enables organic reach at a scale that most platforms no longer permit without paid amplification.
